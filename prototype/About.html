How We Got Here

2022

The team met at a sushi place where they sparked up a conversation on technology and the grandparents of tech. The team decided to create a site to honor these old inventions.

2023

Raghav started to develop the core structure of the page while Tiffany went around finding pictures of worthy vintage tech. Mariam added her flare by making the site look visually appealing with CSS.

2024

With the core features in place, the team launched an early version of the site to a small audience. Feedback poured in — people loved the mix of nostalgia and education. The team refined content, improved accessibility, and made sure every invention told a story worth remembering.

2025

Timeless Tools officially launched to the public, gaining traction among history buffs, tech fans, and students alike. With ongoing updates and a growing archive of artifacts, the team continues their mission to preserve and showcase the legacy of everyday innovation.
